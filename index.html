M.S.DHONI

About

Mahendra Singh Dhoni born 7 July 1981 is an Indian professional cricketer. He is a right handed batter and a wicket-keeper. Widely regarded as one of the most prolific wicket-keeper-batsmen and captains, he represented the Indian cricket team and was the captain of the side in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in test cricket from 2008 to 2014. Dhoni has captained the most international matches and is the most successful Indian captain. He has led India to victory in the 2011 Cricket World Cup, the 2007 ICC World Twenty20 and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, the only captain to win three different limited overs tournaments. He also led the teams that won the Asia Cup in 2010, 2016 and was a member of the title winning squad in 2018.MS Dhoni will go down as one of the most successful cricket captains of all time. Born in Ranchi, Dhoni struggled a lot before he got a chance to play for the Indian Cricket Team. Dhoni decided to focus on cricket from a very young age. He also has great goalkeeping skills in the game of football and his school coach asked him if he would keep the wickets for the cricket team. Dhoni agreed and from there onwards, the historic journey of Mahi began. He also has a biopic to his name with the title, MS Dhoni: The Untold Story

M.S.Dhoni IPL profile

MS Dhoni is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Chennai franchise in the Indian Premier League. MS Dhoni was born on Jul 07, 1981; and as of 2024, he is 43 years old. MS Dhoni is a wicket keeper from Ranchi and bats right handed. He made his IPL debut in 2008.MS Dhoni has played 250 matches to date in his IPL career and has scored 5082 runs , with an average of 39.09. He has also slammed 24 half-centuries, with his highest IPL score being 84* runs. MS Dhoni has hit 349 fours and 239 sixes in his IPL career.In the IPL Auction 2024, the Chennai franchise acquired the services of MS Dhoni for Rs 12.00 Cr .On May 2023, he played his last IPL match against Gujarat at the Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad, India, scoring 0 runs off 1 balls.

Family

MS Dhoni’s father Pan Singh has a crucial role in his successful life. As a parent, he ensured that his all three children get quality education. However, during MS’s initial playing days, he did not support his younger son to represent his school’s cricket team. The father of the World Cup-winning skipper wanted his son to concentrate on academics to thrive in the future.

In MS Dhoni’s life, his mother Devaki Devi has stood with him through thick and thin. In childhood, Dhoni enjoyed the immense support of his mother to become a cricketer. In 2016, Dhoni sported his mother’s name on the back of his jersey in an ODI match against New Zealand in Visakhapatnam.

In 2008, Sakshi met MS while working as an intern at Taj Bengal, Kolkata. On the last day of her internship, Sakshi met the star cricketer through a common friend. After the meeting, Dhoni asked for Sakshi’s number through the friend and both started their relationship. On July 4, 2010, MS Dhoni married Sakshi in Dehradun after having more than a couple of years of relationship. After marriage, she is a parent to Ziva Dhoni, who was born in February 2015. Sakshi was born in Assam and got her primary education at Welham Girls School in Dehradun. She also holds a degree in hotel management from the Institute of Hotel Management, Aurangabad. During IPL games, Sakshi is seen cheering for Dhoni and his team Chennai Super Kings (CSK) from the stands. The CSK stalwart’s wife remains active on Instagram and has 4.8 million followers.

On February 6, 2015, Sakshi Dhoni gave birth to a baby girl, whom she named Ziva. At the time of her birth, Ziva’s father MS Dhoni didn’t take paternity leave as he was on duty serving the Indian cricket team in the 2015 World Cup. Ziva also has an Instagram account which is managed by her father MS Dhoni and her mother Sakshi Dhoni. So far, she has got 2 million followers on the social networking site.
